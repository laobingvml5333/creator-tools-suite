YouTube Chapters Best Practices

YouTube chapters help viewers navigate your content. The first chapter must start at 0:00, and you need at least 3 chapters with a minimum of 10 seconds each.

Chapter Requirements

  • Start at 0:00: First chapter must begin at the video start.
  • Minimum 3 chapters: You need at least three timestamps.
  • 10 seconds minimum: Each chapter should be at least 10 seconds long.
  • Format: Use MM:SS or HH:MM:SS format followed by the chapter title.
