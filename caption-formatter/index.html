Caption Formats Explained

Different platforms require different caption formats. SRT is the most universal, VTT is used for web videos, and plain text is great for social media posts.

Best Practices

  • TikTok: Short lines (5-7 words max) for readability on mobile.
  • YouTube: Longer lines (10-15 words) work better on desktop.
  • Timing: Captions should appear for 2-3 seconds minimum.
